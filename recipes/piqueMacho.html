Pique macho

Description

El pique macho es un plato típico boliviano abundante de la ciudad de Cochabamba, fácil y rápido de preparar. Las porciones más pequeñas simplemente se llaman piqué; El piqué macho suele ser una porción muy grande que difícilmente puede lograr una sola persona. El piqué macho es tradicionalmente picante..

Ingredients

  • 1 kilogram of beef
  • 6 Vienna sausages
  • 2 sausages
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 kilo of potatoes
  • 2 medium-sized onions
  • 3 medium-sized tomatoes
  • 2 Locotos (or jalapenos)
  • Beer
  • Salt
  • Cumin
  • Pepper
  • Soy sauce
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • Oil
  • Llajua (spicy sauce)

Steps

  1. Boil the chorizo in a pot of water to release the fat.
  2. Boil the eggs in the same pot.
  3. Peel and slice the onions and potatoes (into half-moon shapes).
  4. Cut the beef into pieces and place it in a bowl, season with a little beer, salt, cumin, pepper, soy sauce, and the garlic clove, and marinate the meat for one hour.
  5. Cut the Vienna sausages and the chorizos into medium-sized pieces; roast in oil in a pan.
  6. In a pan, sauté the seasoned beef and add the Vienna sausages and pieces of chorizo.
  7. Fry potatoes in the pan or in the oven to make crispy French fries.
  8. Serve the fries on a plate and garnish with meat, raw onion, tomato, locoto (or jalapenos), and boiled egg.
